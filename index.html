Conservators Lenox & Astor Reading Group
Wednesday, October 3 | 6 PM
Private Location
Join fellow Conservators for an intimate conversation with award-winning author and journalist Susan Cheever, daughter of the late writer John Cheever. She will lead a discussion on the Pulitzer Prize-winning book The Stories of John Cheever.
 
"As stories go, as compellingly readable narratives of a certain sort of people in a certain time and place—our time and place—John Cheever's stories are, simply, the best."—The Washington Post

A copy of the book will be provided in advance. RSVP to Taylor Dietz at 212.930.0122 or via email.
